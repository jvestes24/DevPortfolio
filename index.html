About Me

I began learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript in high school where I was a member of a student team that updated and maintained our school district's websites through WordPress. I quickly grasped the concepts and joined a club called Business Professionals of America where I participated in competitions on the fundamentals of Web Design. In my first year, I placed 2nd in my region and 5th in the state of Indiana. I also earned a Microsoft MTA: HTML5 Application Development Fundamentals Certification demonstrating my proficiency in HTML and CSS applications. This experience made me fall in love with programming and I went on to pursue a bachelor's degree of science majoring in Web Programming & Design at Purdue University. At Purdue, I continued learning programming technologies such as PHP, SQL, .NET, and jQuery. It has become my passion to learn new technologies and I'm eager to get my start in the tech industry.
